ABOUT

ABOUT DEHRADUN CITY

Dehradun City


Dehradun, the capital of Uttrakhand is a beautiful city North India, situated at the foothills of the himalayas. Dehradun is a fine blend of a busy city and a picturesque hill station. Famously known as gateway to well known hilly areas of Mussoorie and garhwal, Dehadun is one of the oldest Indian cities. It is also recognized as the hub of education of North India. The city has been ruled by mughal, sikh as well as gorkha rulers in the past. Later on, several educational Institutions of repute were established in this city. Tapkeshwar temple, Buddha temple, Shiv mandir on Mussoorie road, Gun hill, Nag devta temple, Bhadraj temple and Surkhanda Devi are some of the well-known places to visit.



ABOUT DIT UNIVERSITY

DIT University


DIT University, a flagship organization of the Unison group, is a leading University in Uttrakhand. We offer undergraduate, postgraduate and phD programs in several streams of engineering, technology, management, architecture, pharmacy and applied sciences. Mussoorie, the queen of hills is just 20km from DIT University.



DEPARTMENT OF CHEMISTRY

Department of chemistry, DIT

DIT infuses a 20-years centre of excillence with a distinctly non-traditional approach to the undergraduate education. In harmony withthe overall objectives of the University, the primary objective of the Chemistry Department is to develop desire, ability and ambition of students to apply the theoritical and technical knowledge for sustainable and analytical solutions to the problems of scientific and social interests. Department is also fulfilling the responsibility of developing outstanding intellectuals by imparting guidance and mentoring them.



DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

Department of civil engineering, DIT

The Civil Engineering Department of the University was started in the year 2009 offering Under Graduate course in Civil Engineering with an intake capacity of 60 students, which has now been increased to 180. An M. Tech. program in Structural Engineering is also being offered. The department has well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty members and lab technicians. The Department has state-of-the-art laboratories and modern infrastructure.
