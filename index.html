ABOUT DEHRADUN CITY

Dehradun is the capital of Uttrakhand, a hilly North Indian state. Situated at the foothills of the himalayas, Dehradun is a beautiful city which is fine blend of a busy city and a picturesque hill station. Famously known as gateway to well known hilly areas of Mussoorie and garhwal, Dehadun is one of the oldest Indian cities. It is also recognized as the hub of education of North India. The city has been ruled by mughal, sikh as well as gorkha rulers in the past. Later on, several educational Institutions of repute were established in this city. Tapkeshwar temple, Buddha temple, Shiv mandir on Mussoorie road, Gun hill, Nag devta temple, Bhadraj temple and Surkhanda Devi are some of the well-known places to visit.

DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL ENGINEERING

The Civil Engineering Department of the University was started in the year 2009 offering Under Graduate course in Civil Engineering with an intake capacity of 60 students, which has now been increased to 180. CALL FOR RESEARCH PAPERS AND POSTERS

Original Research paper can be submitted in the form of oral or poster presentation. It is the responsibility of the author to confirm that the work has not been presented earlier in the same form or as major component of the presentation ai any other such conference. Presenter will be given 7 minutes to present their research work and 3 minutes for discussion if selected for an oral presentation. Selected papers will be published in the conference proceedings/gournal.


SUBMISSION OF PAPERS

Participants should submit the abstract not exceeding 200 words (Min. 150 words) on a seperate sheet by 30th August 2018. The paper received after the due date will not br considered. The abstract should be typed on Times New Roman, 12 Pt, line spacing 1. The title of the paper should be bold, in capital letters and with a font size of 14 followed by the name of author(s) font size 12 along with their affiliation (italic, font size 12) and the presenting author should be underlined.
